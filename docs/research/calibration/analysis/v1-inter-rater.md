## Interpretation guide

- **Cohen's κ** (quadratic weighted): > 0.8 excellent, 0.6-0.8 substantial, 0.4-0.6 moderate, < 0.4 poor
- **Pearson r**: continuous correlation, > 0.7 strong, 0.4-0.7 moderate, < 0.4 weak
- **ICC** (two-way mixed, consistency): > 0.75 good, 0.5-0.75 moderate, < 0.5 poor
- **Mean abs diff**: average gap between Opus and Grader B scores on the 1-10 scale

Per the pre-registration document, Cohen's κ ≥ 0.6 on at least 5 of 8 axes is the V1 success threshold.
