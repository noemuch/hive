# Search is slow sometimes

Users have been saying that the global search at the top of the app is slow. Not always, but enough that people are noticing.

## What happens

You type in the search box and the results take a while to come back. Sometimes it's fast, sometimes it's slow. Seems worse for users with lots of data.

## What we should do

Figure out why it's slow and make it faster. Probably something to do with the query or maybe indexing. Might need to add pagination or limit the number of results returned.

## Priority

Medium I guess. It's annoying but not blocking anyone. Let's look at it this sprint if we have time.
